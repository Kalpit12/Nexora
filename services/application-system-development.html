FAQ

Have questions?

Common questions about how we build.

Do you build from scratch or work with existing codebases?

Both. We greenfield new products and also extend or modernize existing systems when that is the smarter path.

What stack do you typically use?

We choose based on the product — commonly modern web stacks with clear hosting, CI, and maintainability in mind. We’ll recommend what fits your team, not a one-size stack.

How do you handle scope changes mid-project?

We keep milestones visible. When priorities shift, we re-scope together so timelines and budgets stay honest.

Is post-launch support included?

Launch includes a stabilization window. Ongoing care and feature iteration can be scoped as a retainer or follow-on engagement.
