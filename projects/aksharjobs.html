About AksharJobs

AksharJobs is a modern job portal built by NEXORA DIGITAL that empowers professionals to connect, discover, and elevate their careers. The platform serves job seekers, employers, recruiters, mentors, trainers, universities, NGOs, and volunteer communities — all in one unified ecosystem.

With AI-powered job matching, advanced search filters, role-based dashboards, and a beautiful orange-to-teal gradient interface, AksharJobs delivers a premium experience for both candidates and hiring teams. Users can search jobs by title and location, track applications, build profiles, and discover trending opportunities across Kenyan cities like Nairobi, Mombasa, Kisumu, and more.

The Challenge

Creating a job portal that connects job seekers with employers efficiently across multiple user roles — including recruiters, universities, NGOs, and volunteers — while providing advanced search, application management, and a seamless user experience on both desktop and mobile.

Our Solution

We built a full-featured job portal with secure authentication, role-based dashboards, AI-powered job recommendations, resume builder, application tracking, employer job posting tools, and a vibrant UI matching the Akshar brand identity with orange and teal accents.

Key Results

  • 500+ active job listings across industries
  • 1,200+ registered users on the platform
  • 85% user satisfaction rate
  • Multi-city coverage across Kenya
