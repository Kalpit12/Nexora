About AxarEvents

AxarEvents is a modern event discovery and booking platform built by NEXORA DIGITAL. It connects attendees with career fairs, university events, conferences, expos, and networking experiences — designed specifically for the African market with a mobile-first, premium UI.

The platform goes beyond “tickets” — it supports the full event lifecycle: discovery, bookings, exhibitor management, logistics, and on-site operations, including instant registration and check-ins.

The Challenge

Build a premium, mobile-first events platform tailored for Africa — supporting everything from public discovery and ticketing to exhibitor operations, event logistics, and on-site registration/check-ins.

Our Solution

We built AxarEvents with event discovery, bookings, venue pages, tourist attractions, and a powerful exhibitor portal — plus instant registration/check-ins, interactive 3D floor plans for expo layouts, printing services for event materials, and travel add-ons like flight booking.

Key Results

  • Improved event discovery and booking UX
  • Faster on-site operations with instant registration & QR check-ins
  • Better exhibitor experience with a dedicated portal
  • Richer event experiences via travel and tourism add-ons
