FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Got questions? We've got answers. Here are the most common questions we receive.

How long does it take to build a website?

Project timelines vary based on complexity. A simple website typically takes 2-4 weeks, while complex web applications can take 8-12 weeks. We'll provide a detailed timeline during our initial consultation.

What's included in your web development service?

Our service includes: responsive design, custom development, SEO optimization, performance optimization, security implementation, content management system setup, and 30 days of post-launch support.

Do you provide website maintenance after launch?

Yes! We offer flexible maintenance packages including regular updates, security patches, backups, performance monitoring, and technical support. We also provide 30 days of free support after launch.

Can you work with our existing website?

Absolutely! We can redesign, rebuild, or enhance your existing website. We'll analyze your current site and provide recommendations for improvements while preserving your brand identity.

What technologies do you use?

We use modern, industry-standard technologies including React, Node.js, Python, PHP, WordPress, and various databases. We choose the best stack for each project based on requirements and scalability needs.

How much does a website cost?

Pricing depends on project scope and complexity. Simple websites start from KES 50,000, while complex web applications can range from KES 120,000 to KES 500,000+. We also offer custom pricing as per client request. We provide detailed quotes after understanding your requirements.

Do you offer hosting services?

Yes, we can set up and manage hosting for you, or we can work with your existing hosting provider. We recommend reliable hosting solutions that ensure fast performance and uptime.

What if I'm not satisfied with the result?

We offer a 100% satisfaction guarantee. If you're not happy with the final result, we'll work with you to make it right, or provide a full refund. Your success is our priority.
