FAQ

Answers before you book

Clear answers about timelines, pricing, and how we work — so you can decide with confidence.

Let's talk →
How long does it take to build a website?

Timelines depend on scope. A focused marketing site typically takes 2–4 weeks; growth sites and stores often 4–8 weeks; product / web apps can take 8–12+ weeks. You’ll get a clear timeline in the kickoff scope.

How much does a website cost?

Marketing sites start from KES 50,000, growth sites / stores from KES 120,000, and product / web apps from KES 250,000. Final quotes are scoped to your requirements — need something outside these bands? We’ll provide a custom estimate.

What’s included in a typical engagement?

Responsive design, custom development, foundational SEO setup, performance and security basics, CMS or admin where needed, and 30 days of post-launch support. Exact deliverables are listed in your scope.

Can you work with our existing website?

Yes. We can redesign, rebuild, or enhance what you already have — preserving brand equity while improving structure, speed, and conversion.

Do you provide maintenance after launch?

Yes. Every launch includes 30 days of support. After that we offer flexible care packages for updates, security, backups, monitoring, and ongoing iteration.

What if I’m not satisfied with the result?

We work in visible loops with clear milestones so feedback lands early. If something is off, we fix it within the agreed scope — your success is the point of the engagement.
